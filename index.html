Ash Ketchum

Ash is one of the most famous fictional characters in the world. Ash is the determined and adventurous protagonist of the Pokémon Series. He travels the world aiming to become a Pokémon Master, forming strong bonds with his Pokémon along the way.

Here is Ash:

Ash Ketchum Traits:

Positivity
Ash is super positive and has an optimistic outlook in life which helps him not give up.
Determined
Ash is extremely determined and works hard to accomplish his goal of becoming a Pokemon Master.
Compassionate
Ash is compassionate. He takes care of his friends and pokemon with care. He does not discriminate between the strong and weak pokemon.
